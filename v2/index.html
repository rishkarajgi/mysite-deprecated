Who am I

I have spent the last three years developing software solutions for various businesses and also have experience developing my own product end to end and shipping it to the market. I believe good software takes the commitment of customers, users and technology specialists. Communication is key to getting results that benefit the business. Being fluent in over 5 programming languages I bring to the table a broad portfolio of technologies. Having developed software solutions totalling over hundreds of lines of code I have learnt how important it is to manage stakeholders, to understand what they need and give them what they need. My interests are in solving complex scale problems serving millions (or billions) of customers, high transactions throughput, low latency, and operational problems in enterprise software products.
